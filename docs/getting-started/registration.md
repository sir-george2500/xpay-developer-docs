# Create Your Account

To start accepting payments with X-Pay, you need a developer account.

## Sign Up

Create your account on our [Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.xpay-bits.com/register).

1. Visit the **X-Pay Dashboard**.
2. Click **Sign Up**.
3. Enter your email and business details.
4. Verify your email address.

## Next Steps

Once you have created your account:

1. Log in to the [Dashboard](https://dashboard.xpay-bits.com).
2. Generate your [API Keys](/docs/getting-started/api-keys).
3. Complete your [KYC Verification](/docs/guides/go-live) to activate live payments.
